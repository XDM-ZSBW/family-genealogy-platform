ğŸ¯ What is This?

This folder contains a digital wiki system for Gladys Klingenberg's memoir, automatically generated from a PDF. The wiki includes:

  • 16 people from Gladys's life
  • 806 images extracted from the memoir
  • Interconnected markdown pages with navigation
  • Searchable index of people, places, and themes

The Source

  • PDF: MyLifeStoryGladys Klingenberg.pdf - A photo-based memoir
  • Pages: ~250 pages of text and images
  • Format: Image-heavy with some text
